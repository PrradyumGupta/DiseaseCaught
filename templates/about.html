About DiseaseCaught

DiseaseCaught is an AI-powered platform designed to assist users in identifying potential health issues based on their symptoms. Our goal is to promote early awareness and support informed decisions by leveraging machine learning models trained on medical data.

Note: DiseaseCaught is not a replacement for a licensed healthcare professional. Always consult a doctor for serious health concerns.
